# Open Source Judaism

A collection of scholarly essays exploring Jewish textual tradition through the lenses of mathematics, computer science, rhetoric, and philosophy. These manuscripts examine how classical rabbinic literature intersects with formal and analytical disciplines, written for curious readers of any background.

## Manuscripts

### Algorithms, Mathematics & Computer Science

- [Algorithms and Data Structures in Jewish Thought](book/algorithms-and-data-structures-in-jewish-thought.md) — Algorithmic thinking applied to Jewish law, ethics, and mysticism.
- [The Hebrew Calendar](book/the-hebrew-calendar.md) — The deterministic algorithm that reconciles solar and lunar cycles.
- [Mathematics of Tractate Kinnim](book/mathematics-of-tractate-kinnim.md) — Bird offerings encoding the pigeonhole principle and NP-completeness.
- [Behavioral Despair in the Talmud](book/behavioral-despair-in-the-talmud.md) — Decision boundaries and Kolmogorov complexity in Talmudic law.
- [Dividing Disputed Property in Rabbinic Literature](book/dividing-disputed-property-in-rabbinic-literature.md) — From Mishnaic claims problems to modern game theory.

### Structure & Rhetoric

- [Chiastic Structures in the Tanach](book/chiastic-structures-in-the-tanach.md) — Inverted parallelism across the Hebrew Bible.
- [Chiastic Structures in Ritual](book/chiastic-structures-in-ritual.md) — Concentric symmetry in Jewish worship.
- [Mastering Rhetoric Through Rabbinic Literature](book/mastering-rhetoric-through-rabbinic-literature.md) — A rhetorical curriculum from Mishnah, Talmud, and Midrash.
- [Tannaitic Chreia Parallels](book/tannaitic-chreia-parallels.md) — A catalog of chreia-like passages organized by Progymnasmata categories.

### Philosophy & Theology

- [Bibliomorphism in Rabbinic Literature](book/bibliomorphism-in-rabbinic-literature.md) — The trinitarian doctrine of Man, Text, and God.
- [Inner Speech in Jewish and Philosophical Thought](book/inner-speech-in-jewish-and-philosophical-thought.md) — Internal dialogue from Bible to cognitive science.
- [Self-Reference in Biblical and Rabbinic Literature](book/self-reference-in-biblical-and-rabbinic-literature.md) — Recursion and reflexivity from formal logic to Torah.
- [Cynic Philosophy and Rabbinic Thought](book/cynic-philosophy-and-rabbinic-thought.md) — Parallels and tensions between Cynic and rabbinic traditions.
- [Minimalism in Rabbinic Literature](book/minimalism-in-rabbinic-literature.md) — The aesthetics of less across legal, ethical, and ritual dimensions.
